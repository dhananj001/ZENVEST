Frequently Asked Questions

How does Zenvest create a financial plan?

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your goals, risk profile, and financial situation. Our advisors then craft a personalized plan, incorporating investments, tax strategies, and more.

What are the fees for your services?

Our fees vary based on the complexity of your financial plan. We offer a transparent fee structure, which we’ll discuss during your free consultation.

How can I start investing with Zenvest?

Simply schedule a consultation with us! We’ll assess your goals and recommend the best investment options, such as SIPs, mutual funds, or other instruments.

Is my money safe with Zenvest?

Yes, your investments are made through SEBI-registered platforms. We never take custody of your money — it's always under your control.

Can I talk to a real advisor?

Yes! All our clients get access to a dedicated human advisor who understands your needs and works with you directly.
