Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Mutual Fund?

A mutual fund is an investment vehicle that pools money from multiple investors to invest in securities like stocks, bonds, and other assets. A professional fund manager manages the fund.

Why should I invest through a mutual fund distributor?

A mutual fund distributor, is a licensed professional or organization that helps, assist and educate investors to choose and invest in mutual funds schemes by various AMCs. It also provides post investment services and support.

Are you AMFI-registered?

Yes we are registered with Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI) and holds a valid ARN (AMFI Registration number). This ensuress we operate under SEBI and AMFI guidelines and follow ethical and compliant practices.

Do I need a Demat account to invest in mutual funds?

No, a Demat account is not required to invest in mutual funds. Investments can be made directly through the distributor via physical or digital mode.

Are mutual fund returns guaranteed?

No, mutual fund returns are subject to market risks. However, with proper planning and scheme selection, mutual funds can offer competitive returns over the long term.

What are the charges involved in mutual fund investing?

There are NO direct charges. As a MFD we offer regular plans.

Can I track my investments?

Yes, we provide access to secure user friendly platform, where you can view portfolio, transaction reports, performance reports and more-anytime, anywhere.

Is my investment safe with a distributor?

Your money is not held by the distributor; it's invested directly with the AMC. The distributor acts as a facilitator, ensuring transparency and proper execution of your investment.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us for free consultation. We will access your financial needs and help you complete kyc and onboarding process to start your investment journey.
